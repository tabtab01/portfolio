Greetings! Here you will find my life, my work, and my photographs. Also, you will discover my newest adventure, Road to Hire.

"Normality is a paved road: It's comfortable to walk, but no flowers grow." --Vincent van Gogh

I am currently participating as a student in the Road to Hire program hosted at Red Ventures. Participating in Road to Hire has been one of my greatest challenges and a challenge that I am so appreciative to be a part of.
